## Top 5 Hooks of the Day

Ranked by relevance to Ethos's creative strategy (identity, pricing, urgency).

1. **"Modern dads are redefining what being a provider means — and it starts with this one thing"** — Identity challenge — This is a direct evolution of Ethos's #1 winner "Don't be the dad without life insurance." The shift from shaming to aspiration ("redefining") is the 2026 update of the same mechanic. Highly adaptable for Meta Feed and UGC formats. Source: [oreateai.com](https://www.oreateai.com/blog/the-rise-of-father-tiktok-a-new-trend-in-parenting-content/535b774318c002fb444587a7c10212ad)

2. **"Forget the five stages of grief — let's talk about the four financial seasons of widowhood"** — Curiosity gap — Unique, highly shareable hook with strong in-community trust signals. Ethos adaptation (one season is preventable) is a natural bridge from grief content to protection intent. Source: [tiktok.com/#widowsoftiktok](https://www.tiktok.com/tag/widowsoftiktok)

3. **"Everything you know about protecting your wealth in 2026 is wrong"** — Contrarian — The #1 contrarian hook format of 2026 applied to wealth protection. Maps directly to Ethos's whole-life vs. term story for the [Whale Signal] high-income audience. Source: [sendshort.ai](https://sendshort.ai/guides/tiktok-hooks/)

4. **"World stop — I just found out how much I should have been investing since my 20s"** — Fear/urgency — Adapts the "World Stop" viral Reels format with a regret + price reveal hook for women 30–45. Ethos version pivots from investing to coverage. Source: [later.com](https://later.com/blog/instagram-reels-trends/)

5. **"Career pivot at 45: what I wish I'd done financially before I made the jump"** — Curiosity gap — High-intent [Older] 40–55 audience in career transition = actively reconsidering all financial decisions including benefits and protection. Ethos can intercept this exact moment. Source: [tiktok.com](https://www.tiktok.com/discover/what-is-a-midlife-career-change)
