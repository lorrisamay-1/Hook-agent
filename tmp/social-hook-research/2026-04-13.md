## Top 5 Hooks of the Day

Ranked by relevance to Ethos's creative strategy (identity, pricing, urgency).

1. **"Dads — here's what happens to your family if something happens to you tonight"** — Fear/urgency — This is a direct analog to Ethos's "Don't be the dad without life insurance" creative. The "tonight" urgency is the differentiator — it collapses the procrastination window. Immediate test candidate for UGC video ads targeting male 30–45.

2. **"Be the mom who has a plan — not just a vibe"** — Identity challenge — Mirrors Ethos's "Be the mom with life insurance" winning format precisely, with added cultural relevance ("vibe" language resonates with the FM target). **FLAG: Direct pattern match to Ethos's existing winning hook mechanic.** Adapt immediately.

3. **"Stop saving money in a 401k until you do this first"** — Contrarian — 5.2M views signal. The "do this first" structure creates curiosity and implies urgency. Adapted for Ethos: "Before you max your 401k, do this first" with the answer being a 10-min term life policy. Strong 25–35 acquisition angle.

4. **"Most people in their 40s have no idea how much life insurance actually costs"** — Price reveal — **FLAG: Direct pattern match to Ethos's "In your 30s? Here's what life insurance costs" format** — just age-shifted. This 40–55 variant is undertested in Ethos's current lineup. High-priority adaptation.

5. **"I lost my spouse and almost lost my home — here's exactly how it happened"** — Fear/urgency — The widow segment shows strong organic engagement around financial consequence content. This hook style could work in prospecting for households without coverage — the implied consequence (losing the home) is concrete and devastating.
